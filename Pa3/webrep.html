Steve Hackett

Steve Hackett’s guitar playing defined the sound of Genesis, blending rock with classical and jazz influences. His innovative style has made him one of the most admired guitarists in progressive rock history.

Jimmy Page

As the founder and lead guitarist of Led Zeppelin, Jimmy Page revolutionized rock music with his virtuoso guitar skills and groundbreaking compositions.
